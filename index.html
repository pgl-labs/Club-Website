What is Peaky Gorilla NFT?

PGLC is the next-generation of NFT's art, designed to bridge the gap between NFT's-Metaverse & the real world.

How can I buy Peaky Gorilla Club NFT?

The first 487 of the special trait is listed on opensea for sale, while the other traits can be minted using the mint option above or by using polygonscan website.

When will the Metaverse Game launch?

Metaverse Garden V1.0 is scheduled to launch in Jun 2022.

Which countries will PGLC Garden accept registrations from?

The PGLC Garden will be available to all Peaky Club Gorilla holders & non holders.

When does the Peaky Gorilla Club generation event start?

Peaky Gorilla's can be minted at anytime using the mint option above.

Are there citizens of any countries excluded from the PGLC Sale?

We don’t dictate who is allowed and who is not, the smart contract deployed is based on decentralised blockchain and anyone can mint PGLC.

Do I require KYC to participate?

We don’t dictate if KYC is required or not, due to the nature of smart contracts.

What purchase methods are accepted?

For the special trait that is listed on OpenSea few coins are accepted, kindly view the collection on OpenSea for further details on accepted coins. Other traits can be minted using MATIC.

What type of NFT is Peaky Gorilla Club?

PGLC is a utility ERC-721 token.

Are the NFT's subject to any kind of lock period?

No, they are distributed straight upon purchase or minting.

What is the maximum supply of GORILLA NFT's?
